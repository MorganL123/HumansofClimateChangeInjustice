Call To Action

With sea levels rising and temperatures increasing, the world and its people are becoming more and more vulnerable to climate change. Luckily, we're not alone in taking action against climate change. Fight against climate change with us!

WHAT YOU CAN DO

Speak UP!!!

You can speak up about climate change by signing petitions. These petitions may help influence politicians and public officials to focus more on finding solutions for climate change. Go to websites like:
  • Change.org
    •
  • Support.Nature.org
    •
  • Environmental-Action.org

    • Take ACTION!!!

    You can take simple steps daily to reduce your carbon footprint.

    • Eat the food you buy and eat less meat.

    • Turn off lights, appliances, heaters, and air conditioners when you are not using them. Save energy.

    • Recycle. We don't want our trash going into landfills since the trash will rot and give off gases like methane.

    • Buy eco-friendly materials like recyclable pencils.

    • Purchase light bulbs that last longer and save more energy overall. Think about installing solar panels to power your home.
